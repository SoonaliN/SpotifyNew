ISHA

Biography

Welcome to the official website of Isha, a renowned singer known for her soulful voice. With a career spanning over 18 years, Isha has captivated audiences around the world with her unique style and musical talent.

Music Journey

From her early days in Chennai, Isha showed a natural inclination towards music She honed her skills in play back singer in music director A.R Raguman and she worked in music composser Ilayaraja. Her breakthrough moment came with the release of her debut album, "Kadhal" which garnered widespread acclaim.

Awards and Recognition

Isha's contributions to the music industry have not gone unnoticed. She has received best singer of 2019 award and received a Tamilnadu government "kalaimamani" Award for her outstanding achievements in the field of music
